Personal Favorite Hike

I would personally say that Pike's Peak via Craggs Campground is my favorite hike so far. It has the perfect combination of beautiful views and challenging inclines.This hike took me 8 hours, and although it was very rough, it was totally worth it when I got to the top (image below).


View from the trail

More about Pike's Peak via Craggs Campground:

  • 13 miles out and back
  • Roughly 4,800 feet of elevation gained
  • Class 2 hike
